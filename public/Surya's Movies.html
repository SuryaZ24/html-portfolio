The Best Movies According to Surya

My top 3 movies of all time

Wall-E

This has been, is and always will be my #1 favorite movie of all time. This is probably when Pixar put in the most amount of effort in their projects, and it shows. The characters, the writing, the animation, the plot; all of it flows extremely well with each other. And a lot of messages are to be learned from here.

Robots

Great voice acting and engagement, also has a few adult jokes here and there.

Dragon Ball Super: Broly

Extremely well done animation for years to come out of an action anime movie.
